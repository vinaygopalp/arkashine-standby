Overview

ProSOIL is a novel microfluidic and Machine Learning based Soil On Chip portable onsite testing device that provides printed results of fertilizer and crop recommendation within 20 minutes. A complete automation in the steps of sample preparation providing a common sample preparation for all tests , Sensory testing system nutrients, pH, electrical conductivity and mapped recommendation unit geotagging of data and AI & ML based algorithms for crop recommendation . The USP of the device is SOIL IN RESULTS OUT. This palmtop sensory system provides onsite decision making on farm with assessment of soil parameters at finger tips, acts fast with zero manual intervention
Features:
Complete Automation, Soil On Chip Technology, A Mapped Recommendation Unit, AI and ML based prediction models for fertilizer and crop recommendations.
