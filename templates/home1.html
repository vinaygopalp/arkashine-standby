About Us

Modern Era Modern Component In Agriculture Industry

A pioneering startup dedicated to revolutionizing agricultural practices through the development of cutting-edge portable soil testing devices. Our innovative technology automates the soil quality testing procedure without compromising accuracy, empowering farmers with precise and timely results to optimize crop yield and environmental sustainability.

More About Us

In the modern era of agriculture, our Organization is dedicated to developing advanced hardware devices designed to cater to the needs of farmers, especially those in remote areas without access to soil test laboratories. Our portable devices can be carried to these remote locations, providing instant and accurate soil test results to empower farmers with the information they need to optimize their agricultural practices. Leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning, our devices provide customized recommendations for soil treatment and crop selection, tailored to the specific conditions of each field. Test results are seamlessly uploaded to a secure cloud platform, allowing for easy access, data storage, and long-term analysis. Farmers can monitor soil health trends and make data-driven decisions over time. We understand the challenges faced by farmers, particularly in remote areas, and strive to develop solutions that are accessible, affordable, and easy to use. We are at the forefront of agricultural technology, continuously researching and developing new methods to improve soil health, increase crop yields, and enhance farm efficiency.

SOILSAATHI

SoilSaathi is a revolutionary AI-integrated product that delivers reliable, effortless, and accurate soil analysis directly on the farm. This portable device, designed like a backpack, allows the entire miniature testing lab to be easily transported to remote and challenging locations where traditional soil testing labs are inaccessible. Our advanced technology provides detailed soil profiles, facilitating smart nutrient management and offering customized crop recommendations for enhanced agricultural productivity.

Key Features of SoilSaathi include:
  • AI Integration: Utilizes artificial intelligence for precise and reliable soil analysis.
  • Portability: Compact and lightweight, designed like a backpack for easy transportation to farms and remote areas.
  • Comprehensive Soil Testing: Measures pH, electrical conductivity (EC), macronutrients, and all micronutrients.
  • Instant Results: Provides immediate soil test results, enabling quick decision-making in 20 minutes.
  • Custom Recommendations: Delivers tailored fertilizer and crop recommendations based on soil profiles of each farm.
  • Rugged Design: Built to withstand harsh field conditions where traditional soil testing labs are inaccessible.
  • No Initial Calibration: Good to go model with no requirement of repetitive calibration.
  • Self Cleaning: The design is equipped with easy cleaning modules within 2 minutes.
  • Cloud Interface: The tested results of soil samples are saved and recorded over cloud through a web interface.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to operate with intuitive navigation controls, displays, and printing units.
  • Real-Time Data Analysis: Offers live data for effective nutrient management and optimal crop planning.
  • Enhanced Productivity: Supports improved agricultural practices by providing detailed soil insights.
  • Sustainability: Promotes sustainable farming through accurate soil health assessments and resource management.
SOILIFE

SoilLife is an innovative chamber designed to be inserted into the soil to accurately measure organic carbon, microbial activity, and greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. Equipped with state-of-the-art sensors, it delivers real-time data analysis of live soil conditions, including temperature, moisture, pH, and electrical conductivity (EC). This precise measurement of carbon emissions enables farmers to effectively manage their carbon credit scores.

Key Features of SoilLife include:
  • Advanced Measurement: Accurately measures organic carbon, microbial activity, and greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.
  • Real-Time Data Analysis: Provides live analysis of soil conditions, including temperature, moisture, pH, and electrical conductivity (EC).
  • Precision Carbon Monitoring: Precisely tracks carbon emissions to help farmers manage their carbon credit scores effectively.
  • IoT Integration: Enables live monitoring of large land areas, facilitating data-driven decision-making.
  • Sustainable Practices: Supports sustainable farming practices, enhancing soil health and productivity.
  • Seamless Integration: Easily integrates with existing farm management systems for comprehensive soil management.
  • User-Friendly Design: Intuitive interface for straightforward operation and data interpretation.
  • Durable Construction: Robust design for reliable performance in various field conditions.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Provides actionable insights for agricultural agencies and B2B businesses.
  • Enhanced Productivity: Contributes to improved soil management and agricultural productivity through advanced monitoring capabilities.
SOILSPARSH

Designed for seamless integration with IoT-based setups and devices, SoilSparsh empowers farmers with real-time data and insights. This enables informed, onsite decision-making for optimizing smart irrigation management systems, ensuring efficient water usage and enhancing agricultural productivity.

Key Features of SoilSparsh include:
  • Comprehensive Environmental Monitoring: Measures moisture, humidity, and temperature for thorough environmental assessment.
  • Portable Design: Compact and lightweight, making it easy to transport and use in various field conditions.
  • Real-Time Data: Provides immediate analysis and feedback for on-the-spot decision-making.
  • WiFi Connectivity: Enables seamless connection to wireless networks for convenient data transfer and remote monitoring.
  • IoT Compatibility: Integrates seamlessly with IoT-based setups and devices for enhanced connectivity and automation.
  • Smart Irrigation Management: Supports intelligent irrigation systems by delivering precise environmental data, optimizing water usage.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple to operate with intuitive controls and displays for ease of use.
  • Durable Construction: Built to withstand harsh outdoor environments and rigorous use.
  • Versatile Application: Suitable for diverse agricultural scenarios, from small farms to large agricultural enterprises.
  • Enhanced Productivity: Helps improve crop yields and resource management through precise environmental monitoring.
  • Sustainable Practices: Promotes efficient water usage and sustainable farming techniques by providing accurate environmental insights.

  • Customized Recommendations: Utilizing AI and machine learning algorithms, the device provides tailored recommendations for soil management practices, optimizing crop yields and resource use.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The device features an intuitive touchscreen interface, making it easy for farmers and agricultural professionals to operate and interpret results.
  • Portable and Durable Design: Built for field conditions, the Mapped Recommendation Unit is portable and rugged, ensuring reliable performance in diverse environments.
  • Data Integration: Results can be integrated with other farm management software, allowing for comprehensive farm management and precision agriculture.
  • Environmental Impact Tracking: By monitoring soil health, our device helps track and reduce the environmental impact of farming practices, promoting sustainable agriculture.
  • Regular Updates: Our team continuously improves the software with regular updates, ensuring the device remains at the cutting edge of soil health technology.

The Mapped Recommendation Unit not only provides valuable insights into current soil conditions but also supports future agricultural planning and sustainability efforts.
